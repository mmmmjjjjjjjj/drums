SOLO MUSIC

Max Jaffe is a drummer, composer, and producer based in Brooklyn, NY.
His work aims to expand the boundaries of possibility for storytelling
and collaboration through the drums. Max’s music draws from many different
genres and seeks to break the boundaries between them. He is part of a cutting edge
of drummers that have embraced Sunhouse and their Sensory Percussion technology and
have helped shift the paradigm of their instrument. His powerful live performances
exploit the physicality of his instrument to create narrative through movement,
defying visual expectations and logic for what a “drum solo” can express.
He is a longtime member of experimental rock collective JOBS Amirtha Kidambi’s Elder Ones,
and has performed internationally with Chrome Sparks, Peter Evans, Rubblebucket, Cass McCombs,
and many others. In 2021 he received an MFA from California Institute of the Arts in
Music Technology and was a Pioneer Works Music Resident.
