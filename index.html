Hey, Max Jaffe here :)

I create improvisatory multimedia tapestries that position the drum set within a continuum of music technology.
Using sensor-based machine learning, digital signal processing, sample-based storytelling, and audio-reactive animations, my work seeks to
render personal meaning out of the impersonal data that surrounds us. I'm fortunate to be a part of a cutting edge of drummers that have
embraced Sunhouse and their Sensory Percussion technology and have helped shift the paradigm of the drums in the process. In my
solo performances, I exploit the physicality of my instrument to create narrative through movement, defying visual expectations
and logic for what a “drum solo” can express. In addition to my solo work, I am a founding member of JOBS and Amirtha Kidambi’s Elder Ones.
