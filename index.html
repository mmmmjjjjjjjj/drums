Max Jaffe

drummer | composer | producer | educator

NY/CA | CalArts MFA 2021 - Music Technology

Hey, Max Jaffe here :)

I create improvisatory multimedia tapestries that position the drum set within a continuum of music technology.
Using sensor-based machine learning, digital signal processing, sample-based storytelling, and audio-reactive animations, my work
renders personal meaning out of the impersonal data that surrounds us. I'm fortunate to be a part of a cutting edge of drummers that have
embraced Sunhouse and their Sensory Percussion technology and have helped shift the paradigm of the drums in the process.
After studying at The New School for Jazz, I based myself in New York and co-founded the band JOBS.
I've performed internationally and recorded with Amirtha Kidambi's Elder Ones, Chrome Sparks, Leverage Models, Peter Evans, and others.
