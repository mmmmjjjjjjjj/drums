Hi! Welcome to the reel page. There's no button for this page, so I probably sent you this link :)
In addition to drumming, I also produce, mix, edit, and compose for other media.
Here is a sampling of recent works.


La Bola

I used my cell phone to record the rhythms of a generator backstage at a festival I was playing in Madrid
I converted the layers to MIDI and out of that came the song below



Worlds Inside My Mind

Composed and produced for Caitlin Javech's MFA Choreography Thesis research at CalArts
The singular dancer in this piece evoked an innocence amidst a tempestuous experience that I tried to highlight



Here/Not Here

Composed and produced for CalArts Digital Expo with MFA Choreographer Taylor Donofrio
With a one minute time limit on the video, we explored multiple timelines through movement and music



Bar Jai Ca

Featured on John Tejada's Future Stars Compilation
Made for fun using Ableton Live



Lionness (MC Yallah, prod. by Debmaster)

Drumming and mixing for this special collaboration
MC Yallah (Kenya/Uganda) x Debmaster (EU) + Max Jaffe (CA)
